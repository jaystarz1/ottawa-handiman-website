Common Questions About Our Ottawa Woodworking Service

Where can I get wood cut to size in Ottawa?

Our Barrhaven workshop offers precision wood cutting for the entire Ottawa area. Unlike big-box stores, we guarantee accurate cuts every time with professional-grade tools and experienced craftsmanship.

Do you take small woodworking jobs in Ottawa?

Absolutely! We specialize in small jobs that larger contractors won't take. Our $100 minimum covers most small cutting and repair projects. No project is too small for our workshop.

Can you help with my DIY project materials?

Yes! Bring your lumber (from The WoodSource or anywhere else) and your plans - we'll handle the precision cutting and assembly. Save money on tools you'll rarely use.

Why choose a small job shop over big stores?

Professional-grade tools, experienced craftsmanship, and personal attention to detail you won't get at hardware stores. We guarantee precision and take pride in every cut.

What's your service area in Ottawa?

We serve the entire Ottawa region from our Barrhaven workshop. Customers come from Kanata to Orleans, Nepean to Gloucester - anywhere in the Ottawa area.

How does the drop-off service work?

Simply call to discuss your project, bring your materials to our workshop (address provided when scheduling), and pick up your finished work in 2-3 days. It's that easy!

Do you offer emergency or weekend woodworking services?

Yes! We understand projects don't always happen on weekdays. Call 343.987.0900 for weekend availability and rush job options. We're here when Ottawa needs us.

What's the difference between your service and Home Depot wood cutting?

Night and day! We offer precision cuts with professional tools, complex angles, custom shapes, and guaranteed accuracy. Home Depot only does basic straight cuts with often poor results.
